Al-Tawilat

Jebel Al-Tawilat in Dahab, South Sinai, is known for its striking red and gold rock formations. It offers stunning views of the Gulf of Aqaba and is popular for rock climbing and serene sunsets. The mountain provides a peaceful escape from city life.
